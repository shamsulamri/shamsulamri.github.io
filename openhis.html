OpenHIS

Hospital Information System

This is an open source hospital information system designed for small or large facility. It contains all the essential modules for running a hospital from patient registration to billing. Built entirely on top of open source technologies to ensure sustainability and great support from community.

View this project on Github.

Special Feature



Web-based application for fast deployment and easy access.




Consultation notes with custom clinical pathways.




Coded operational dataset ready to use from drugs, consumable items to consultation fees.




Possible drug prescription options built into the order function.


Modules

  • Patient registration, appointment & scheduling
  • Admission, Queue, Discharge and Transfer
  • Clinical Consultation
  • Order Management
  • Ward & Bed Management
  • Diet & Kitchen Management
  • Inventory & Stock management
  • Drug Information & Prescription
  • Custom Forms
  • Patient Billing
  • Medical Record
  • Reports & Print-outs

Training Scripts

OpenHIS uses a custom training application to train users on how to use the application. Users will follow a set of instructions to peform daily task such as patient registration, consultation or bill creation. Progress of each user will be recorded and their proficiency can be monitored by supervisors. The training application can be accessed at:

https://dojo.iodojo.com

The scripts are a bit rough, going through updating process right now.
