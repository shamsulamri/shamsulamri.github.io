Training Scripts

OpenHIS uses a custom training application to gauge the performance of the users. The training application contain scripts where the user can follow to complete certain task such as patient registration, clinical consultation or generate bills for the patient. The training module encourage users to learn and progress at their own pace. Their progress can later be analysed and reviewed by administrators.
