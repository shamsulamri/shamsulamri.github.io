OpenHIS

This is an open source hospital information system designed for small or large facility. It contains all the essential modules for running a hospital from patient registration to billing. Built entirely on top of open source technologies to ensure sustainability and great support from community.

View this project on Github.

Highlight Features



Web-based application for fast deployment and easy access.




Enter consultation notes with custom clinical pathways.




Coded clinical dataset ready to use from drugs, consumable items to consultation fees.


Modules

  • Patient registration, appointment & scheduling
  • Admission, Queue, Discharge and Transfer
  • Clinical Consultation
  • Order Management
  • Ward & Bed Management
  • Diet & Kitchen Management
  • Inventory & Stock management
  • Drug Information & Prescription
  • Custom Forms
  • Patient Billing
  • Medical Record

Open Source

The application was designed and built entirely on open source techonologies.
